About Simpliby

Simplifying your shopping experience

We know you can do a lot with your time, so we can't afford to watch you waste it while searching for an item or queueing in a store.

Our Mission

To give everyone the ability to shop at their convinence and without limitation.

Our Motivation

Before

Shopping was becoming so difficult and crowdy, many persons avoided shopping due to the hassle that comes with it. While some transitioned into trusting delivery services and eventually had an awful experience

The pain

Individuals having to stand on a long queue, delay in locating items to be purchased and lack of privacy in purchasing some certain products and we also saw how this affected most store owners to close on time, being rude to their customers and a loss of products due to theft as a result of crowded store.

After

Simpliby is a solution created that allows it's users to purchase any item, reserve them and set a time at which they intend to pick it up paying either cash or via the app. It also allows Store owners to generate commisions for reserving items.
