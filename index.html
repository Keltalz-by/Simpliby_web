Who we are

Simpliby is a cutting-edge technology that is transforming the way we shop. Our user-friendly app is available on mobile and web platforms, allowing anyone with an internet connection to easily browse through a wide range of products in selected malls. With Simpliby, shoppers can reserve items and pick them up at their convenience, eliminating the need to physically visit a mall and saving time for busy professionals.

One of the key benefits of Simpliby is its focus on safety and security. Our app uses state-of-the-art encryption technology to protect user data and ensure secure transactions. Additionally, our 24/7 customer support team is always available to assist with any issues or concerns.

A unique feature of Simpliby is that it allows sellers and store owners to get paid for reserving an item, increasing the availability of items and improving the shopping experience for users. This commitment from sellers ensures that the item will be waiting for the buyer when they arrive for pickup. Simpliby is dedicated to making shopping simple, hassle-free, and secure, providing a convenient solution for modern shoppers.

You have questions?

We have answers

How does Simpliby work?

Simpliby is a user-friendly app available on both mobile and web platforms that allows you to browse and reserve products from your local malls. You can select and reserve items through the app, and then pick them up at your convenience.

Is Simpliby safe for online shopping?

Yes, Simpliby uses state-of-the-art encryption technology to protect user data and ensure that all transactions are secure. We also have a dedicated customer support team available 24/7 to assist with any issues or concerns.

How do I pay for my reserved items on?

You can pay for your reserved items using the secure payment options available on the app. We accept major credit cards and other forms of electronic payment.

Can I reserve items from multiple stores?

Yes, you can reserve items from multiple sellers or stores on Simpliby. Simply browse through the available products from different sellers or stores in your selected mall and reserve the items you want from each seller or store.

How do sellers get paid for reserving items?

Sellers on Simpliby receive payment for reserved items once the buyer picks up the item from the store. This ensures that the seller is compensated for holding the item for the buyer.

Is Simpliby available in all locations?

Simpliby is currently available in select malls and locations. You can check the app for a list of available malls and stores in your area.
